The Power of Semantic HTML

Semantic HTML elements are crucial for accessibility and SEO. They provide meaning to the structure of your content, making it easier for both browsers and assistive technologies to understand the purpose of different parts of your webpage.

Using elements like `header`, `nav`, `article`, `aside`, and `footer` helps define the role of content within the document, leading to a more robust and maintainable codebase.
